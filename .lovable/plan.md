
## 2FLY® Storefront Replica

Build a static e-commerce storefront UI replicating the 2FLY® clothing store from the screenshot.

### Pages & Layout

**Header (all pages)**
- Search icon (left), "2FLY®" logo text (center), account icon + cart/bag icon (right)
- Clean white background, minimal style

**Home / Product Listing Page**
- 2-column product grid on mobile, 3-4 columns on desktop
- Each product card: product image on white background, product name, price in KSh format
- "Sold out" badge overlay on unavailable items
- Products to include (hardcoded data):
  - Cropped hoodie — KSh5,000.00 (Sold out)
  - Cropped Unisex tee — KSh1,500.00
  - Croptops (pink) — KSh1,500.00
  - Croptops (black) — KSh1,000.00
  - Additional items in pink and light blue

**Product Detail Page**
- Large product image, title, price
- Size selector, "Add to Cart" button
- Clean minimal layout

**Cart Drawer/Page**
- Slide-out cart or separate page showing added items
- Quantity controls, total price, "Checkout" button (non-functional)

### Design
- Clean white background, black text
- Minimal Shopify-like aesthetic
- Sans-serif typography
- Product images on plain white backgrounds
- Responsive: mobile-first grid layout matching the screenshot
